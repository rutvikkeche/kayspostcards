# Kay's Postcards

> A quiet little corner of the internet.

This is a simple, handcrafted website I built to send personal postcards to someone special - no social feeds, no noise, just a letter, a photo, and a date. Each postcard is a small moment I wanted her to be able to come back to whenever she wants.

The design is intentional - warm paper tones, serif type, muted ink; because the words deserve a home that feels as careful as they are. No flashy UI, no unnecessary features. Just a cozy, permanent reminder, forever etched on the internet, that she is extraordinary.

It's not built to scale. It's built for one person.
