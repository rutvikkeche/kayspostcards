4 aventures de Reinette et Mirabelle

The title women and fiction might mean, and you may have meant it to mean, women and what they are like; or it might mean women and the fiction that they write: or it might mean women and the fiction that is written about them; or it might mean that somehow all three are inextricably mixed to-gether and you want me to consider them in that light.
